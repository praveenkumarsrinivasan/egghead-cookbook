Leg of Lamb Recipe for Big Green Egg

Recipe type: Main Cuisine: European

Perp time: 160 min 
Cook time: 180 min 
Total time: 340 min

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

VEGETABLES:
2ea Parsnips
2ea Red beetroots
2ea Yellow beetroots
2ea Potatoes (Baby Purple Kumara)
2tbsp Olive oil

LEG OF LAMB:
1ea Small leg of lamb
2tbsp Olive oil
Your Desired rub for lamb

Directions:

PREPARATION IN ADVANCE:
Cut off any loose skin and fat from the leg of lamb then Rub the leg of lamb with olive oil and coat
firmly with the rub you have selected. Leave to marinate in the refrigerator for 1.5 hours.
Remove the leg of lamb from the refrigerator half an hour before you intend roasting it. Ignite the
charcoal in the Big Green Egg and heat, with the convEGGtor and the grid, to a temperature of 355-
400°F. Meanwhile, wash the vegetables but don’t peel or cut them, peel and finely chop the garlic.

COOKING PREPARATION:
Place the leg of lamb on the grid. Push the Probe Thermometer into the core of the meat. Close the lid
of the EGG and set the temperature of the core thermometer. Maintain 130-136°F (55-58°C) for
medium-rare (only if using a boneless rolled leg of lamb), maintain 140-145°F (60-63°C) for medium and 149-
152°F (65-67°C) for medium to well done.

Sprinkle the vegetables with olive oil, add the finely chopped garlic &amp; salt. When the core temperature
of the leg of lamb is about 77°F (25°C) below the desired core temperature. Place the vegetables on the
grid next to the leg of lamb and leave to roast for about 1 hour.

Remove the leg of lamb and the vegetables from the EGG. Halve or quarter the vegetables and serve
them on an attractive board together with the leg of lamb.
