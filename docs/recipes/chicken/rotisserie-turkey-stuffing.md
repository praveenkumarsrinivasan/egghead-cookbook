# Rotisserie Turkey and Stuffing

## Overview

Herb-heavy turkey prep with a cream-cheese base and simple aromatic stuffing.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Rotisserie turkey
- Cuisine: American
- Prep time: 45 minutes + overnight rest
- Cook time: varies by bird size
- Total time: 1-2 days
- Serves: 8-12
- EGG setup: rotisserie
- EGG target temperature: moderate roasting heat

## Ingredients

Turkey:

- 1 turkey (12-15 lb)

Herb cream cheese:

- 16 oz cream cheese, softened
- 1 bundle oregano, chopped
- 1 bundle sage, chopped
- 1 bundle thyme, chopped
- 1 bundle rosemary, chopped

Stuffing aromatics:

- 1 apple, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced

## Method

1. The day before, mix cream cheese with chopped herbs; refrigerate.
2. The day of, loosen the skin and spread herb cream cheese underneath.
3. Stuff the cavity with apple and onion slices.
4. Rotisserie roast until the turkey is cooked through; rest before carving.

## Notes

- Step 4 is inferred; the source focuses on prep for rotisserie cooking.
