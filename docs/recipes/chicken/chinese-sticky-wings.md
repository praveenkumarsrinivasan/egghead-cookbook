# Chinese Sticky Wings

## Overview

Sweet, sticky wings glazed with hoisin, soy sauce, and honey.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Grilled wings
- Cuisine: Chinese-inspired
- Prep time: 10 minutes + marinating time
- Cook time: 30-45 minutes
- Total time: 1-2 hours
- Serves: 4-6
- EGG setup: direct or indirect
- EGG target temperature: medium-high

## Ingredients

Wings:

- 2 lb chicken wings

Sauce:

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup hoisin sauce
- 1 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup honey

## Method

1. Whisk sauce ingredients together and reserve some for basting.
2. Marinate wings, then cook over steady heat until cooked through.
3. Baste with the sauce as the wings finish to build a sticky glaze.
