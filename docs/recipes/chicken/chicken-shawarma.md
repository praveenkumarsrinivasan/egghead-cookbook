# Chicken Shawarma

## Overview

Smoky, spiced chicken thighs grilled or rotisserie cooked and served with classic sides.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Grilled chicken
- Cuisine: Middle Eastern-inspired
- Prep time: 10 minutes + marinating time
- Cook time: 20-40 minutes
- Total time: 1-2 hours
- Serves: 4-6
- EGG setup: direct or rotisserie
- EGG target temperature: medium-high

## Ingredients

Chicken:

- 2.5 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Marinade:

- 3 Tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 lemon, zest and juice
- 1 Tbsp chopped garlic

Spice mix:

- 1 Tbsp paprika
- 1 Tbsp turmeric
- 1 Tbsp cumin
- 1 Tbsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper

## Method

1. Mix olive oil, lemon, garlic, and spices; coat chicken and marinate.
2. Grill directly or cook on a rotisserie until well browned and cooked through.
3. Rest briefly, slice, and serve with rice, salad, and naan if desired.
