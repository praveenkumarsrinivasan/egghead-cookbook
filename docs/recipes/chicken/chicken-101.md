# Chicken 101

## Overview

Spatchcocked chicken with an overnight dry brine and a tangy Alabama white sauce.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Whole chicken
- Cuisine: American BBQ
- Prep time: 20 minutes + overnight dry brine
- Cook time: 1-2 hours
- Total time: 1-2 days
- Serves: 4-6
- EGG setup: direct or indirect (focus on clean fire and crisp skin)
- EGG target temperature: medium-high

## Ingredients

Chicken:

- 1 whole chicken

Dry brine:

- 2 Tbsp kosher salt
- 2 tsp white sugar
- 3/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cornstarch

Alabama white sauce:

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
- 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 Tbsp prepared horseradish
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper

Optional: Lawry's alternative

- 2 Tbsp kosher salt
- 2 tsp white sugar
- 3/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cornstarch

## Method

1. Spatchcock the chicken and dry brine overnight.
2. Set up the EGG for clean, steady heat that encourages crisp skin.
3. Cook until the breast and thighs are done to your preference.
4. Rest briefly, then serve with Alabama white sauce.
