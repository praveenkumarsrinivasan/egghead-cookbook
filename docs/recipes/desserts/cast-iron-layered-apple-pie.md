# Cast Iron Layered Apple Pie

## Overview

A layered apple pie baked in cast iron with spiced apples and buttery crust.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Dessert
- Cuisine: American
- Prep time: 20 minutes
- Cook time: 45 minutes
- Total time: 1 hour 5 minutes
- Serves: 8-10
- EGG setup: indirect
- EGG target temperature: 350 F

## Ingredients

Crust and filling:

- 2 packages Pillsbury pie crust
- 8 Granny Smith apples, cored and sliced
- 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup white sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp clove
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 cup butter

## Method

1. Preheat the EGG to 350 F.
2. Peel, core, and slice the apples.
3. Combine flour, sugars, and spices; toss with apples and lemon juice.
4. Butter a cast iron pan and layer crust, apples, and butter.
5. Repeat layers, finishing with crust on top.
6. Bake for about 45 minutes until golden.
