## Recipe Essentials

Serves: 8

- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 40 minutes
- Total time: 50 minutes
- EGG setup: indirect, then direct for sear
- EGG target temperature: 350F indirect, 500-600F for sear

## Ingredients

- 1.7 kg tri tip
- Freshly ground NY cut pepper
- Flaky sea salt or kosher salt (smoked sea salt works too)
- Smoked paprika
- Garlic powder
- Optional: your favorite rub

Blue cheese smoked paprika butter:

- 4 oz blue cheese
- 1 stick butter, softened
- Fresh ground pepper
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika

## Method

1. Prepare the EGG for 350F indirect heat using the deflector.
2. Season the tri tip with salt, pepper, garlic, paprika, or your favorite rub.
3. Smoke on indirect heat for 20-30 minutes, until internal temperature reaches 120-127F.
4. Rest under a foil tent.
5. Raise the EGG temperature to 500-600F and sear each side for about 2 minutes, aiming for 127-130F for medium rare.
6. Rest for 10 minutes, slice, and serve with the blue cheese butter.

## Notes

- The thinner end cooks faster; monitor internal temperature closely.
- This roast is best cooked to medium rare rather than well done.
