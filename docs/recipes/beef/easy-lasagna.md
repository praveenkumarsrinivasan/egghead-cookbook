# Easy Lasagna

## Overview

A hearty, cheese-forward lasagna built with ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan, and a beefy tomato sauce.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Baked pasta
- Cuisine: Italian-American
- Prep time: 25 minutes
- Cook time: 45-60 minutes
- Total time: 1.5 hours
- Serves: 8-10
- EGG setup: indirect
- EGG target temperature: moderate baking heat

## Ingredients

Cheese layer:

- 32 oz ricotta cheese
- 1 lb fresh mozzarella
- 16 oz low moisture mozzarella
- 2 cups grated parmesan
- 2 eggs
- 1/4 cup fresh basil

Meat sauce:

- 1.5 lb ground beef, browned
- 2 Tbsp garlic, minced
- 2 cups pasta sauce
- 1 can stewed tomatoes
- 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 Tbsp garlic powder

Pasta:

- 1 package lasagna noodles

## Method

1. Mix ricotta, eggs, basil, and half the parmesan.
2. Combine browned beef with garlic, pasta sauce, stewed tomatoes, and seasonings.
3. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a greased baking dish.
4. Repeat layers and finish with remaining mozzarella and parmesan.
5. Bake until bubbling and the top is well browned; rest before slicing.
