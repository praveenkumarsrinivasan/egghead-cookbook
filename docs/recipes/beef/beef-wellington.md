# Beef Wellington

## Overview

Chateaubriand wrapped with mushroom duxelles and prosciutto, finished in puff pastry.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Beef Wellington
- Cuisine: European
- Prep time: 1-2 hours + overnight dry brine
- Cook time: until pastry is golden and center reaches desired doneness
- Total time: 1-2 days
- Serves: 6-8
- EGG setup: indirect
- EGG target temperature: moderate baking heat

## Ingredients

Base:

- 1 chateaubriand, dry brined with kosher salt overnight

Tallow injection:

- 2 lb ground beef fat
- 10 whole garlic cloves
- 4 roasted chestnuts, ground
- 8 dried truffles
- 3 sprigs fresh thyme
- 4 cups water

Rub:

- Truff hot sauce (binder)
- 1/2 Tbsp granulated garlic
- 1 Tbsp fresh cracked black pepper

Wellington:

- 2 Tbsp prepared horseradish
- 2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
- 700 g brown cremini mushrooms
- 5 garlic cloves
- 2 tsp smoked sea salt
- 2 tsp fresh cracked black pepper
- 6 roasted chestnuts
- Prosciutto, enough to wrap the roast
- Puff pastry
- 1 egg, beaten

Optional:

- Bourbon for the sear
- Thyme herb smoke bomb

## Method

1. Dry brine the chateaubriand overnight.
2. Render beef fat with garlic, chestnuts, truffles, thyme, and water; strain and inject.
3. Season with the rub and sear (optionally with bourbon); cool.
4. Pulse mushrooms, garlic, chestnuts, salt, and pepper; cook down to a dry duxelles.
5. Brush roast with horseradish and mustard, wrap in prosciutto and duxelles, then in puff pastry.
6. Egg-wash and bake until pastry is deep golden and center reaches desired doneness.
7. Rest before slicing.
