# Pastrami

## Overview

Brisket brined with a custom pickling spice mix, then rubbed and smoked.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Smoked pastrami
- Cuisine: American deli
- Prep time: 30 minutes + multi-day brine
- Cook time: low and slow (varies)
- Total time: several days
- Serves: depends on brisket size
- EGG setup: indirect
- EGG target temperature: low and slow

## Ingredients

Brisket:

- 1 whole brisket (12-14 lb), trimmed

Pickling spice:

- 1 Tbsp black peppercorns
- 1 Tbsp coriander seeds
- 1 Tbsp mustard seeds
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1 tsp fennel seeds
- 1 tsp allspice berries

Brine:

- 2 gallons water
- 4 lb ice
- 1/2 cup pink curing salt
- 1/2 cup kosher salt
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1 Tbsp minced garlic
- 1/2 diced yellow onion
- Pickling spice mix (above)

Rub:

- 1/4 cup black pepper
- 1/4 cup coriander powder
- 2 Tbsp granulated garlic
- 2 Tbsp brown sugar

## Method

1. Toast and lightly crush the pickling spices.
2. Mix the brine, chill, and submerge brisket for several days.
3. Rinse, pat dry, and apply the rub.
4. Smoke indirectly until the bark is set and the brisket is tender.
5. Rest, slice thin, and serve.

- Method is inferred from the ingredient list and standard pastrami process.
