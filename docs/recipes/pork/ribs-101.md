# Ribs 101

## Overview

Hot-and-fast ribs with an overnight dry brine, a clean double-indirect fire, and a light glaze.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Smoked pork ribs
- Cuisine: American BBQ
- Prep time: 15 minutes + overnight dry brine
- Cook time: about 3 hours
- Total time: 1-2 days
- Serves: 4-6
- EGG setup: double indirect
- EGG target temperature: hot and fast

## Ingredients

Ribs:

- 2 racks of ribs

Dry brine:

- 2 Tbsp kosher salt
- 2 tsp white sugar
- 3/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cornstarch

Rib sauce:

- 1 cup Sweet Baby Ray's BBQ sauce
- 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar

Optional: Lawry's alternative

- 2 Tbsp kosher salt
- 2 tsp white sugar
- 3/4 tsp paprika
- 1/4 tsp ground turmeric
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cornstarch

## Method

1. Dry brine ribs overnight.
2. Smoke hot and fast over a clean double-indirect fire.
3. Use a foil boat if needed to protect the bottom bark.
4. Mix rib sauce and vinegar, then glaze near the finish.
5. Rest briefly and serve.
