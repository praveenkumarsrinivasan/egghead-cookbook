# Holy Voodoo Dry Rub

## Overview

A bold, spicy dry rub with black pepper, paprika, garlic, and chili heat.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Dry rub
- Cuisine: BBQ
- Prep time: 5 minutes
- Cook time: none
- Total time: 5 minutes
- Serves: makes enough for multiple cooks

## Ingredients

Rub:

- 1/2 cup black pepper
- 1/4 cup paprika
- 1/4 cup granulated garlic
- 1/4 cup kosher salt
- 2 Tbsp cayenne pepper
- 2 Tbsp red pepper flakes
- 2 Tbsp chili powder

## Method

1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
2. Store in an airtight container.
