# Ultimate Texas SPG Dry Rub

## Overview

A simple salt-pepper-garlic blend for beef and classic Texas-style BBQ.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Dry rub
- Cuisine: BBQ
- Prep time: 5 minutes
- Cook time: none
- Total time: 5 minutes
- Serves: makes enough for multiple cooks

## Ingredients

Rub:

- 1/2 cup black pepper
- 1/2 cup kosher salt
- 1/4 cup granulated garlic

## Method

1. Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
2. Store in an airtight container.
