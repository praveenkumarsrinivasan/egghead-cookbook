## Overview

Savory, spicy, and nutty dipping sauce for grilled meats.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Dipping sauce
- Cuisine: Korean
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Total time: 10 minutes
- Serves: 4

## Ingredients

- 32 g doenjang (fermented soybean paste)
- 21 g gochujang (Korean chili paste)
- 1 scallion, chiffonade
- 32 g onion, minced
- 5 g honey
- 5 g toasted sesame seeds
- 5 g sesame oil
- 2.5 g lemon juice (or lime for a sharper finish)

## Method

1. Add scallion and onion to a small bowl.
2. Add remaining ingredients and mix until fully combined.
3. Transfer to a serving bowl and cover until needed.

Notes: This keeps for up to 7 days in the fridge. Great with BBQ, especially beef and pork.
