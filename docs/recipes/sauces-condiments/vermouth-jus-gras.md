# Vermouth Jus Gras

## Overview

A rich, glossy demi-glace sauce brightened with dry vermouth and cream.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Sauce
- Cuisine: French
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 10 minutes (may vary)
- Total time: 20 minutes
- Yield: About 100 ml (roughly 6 small servings)

## Ingredients

- 30 g salted butter (good quality)
- 1 shallot, fine brunoise
- 45 ml dry vermouth
- 500 g demi-glace cubes (frozen)
- 30 ml full-fat cream
- Kosher salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste

## Method

1. Finely brunoise the shallot and set aside.
2. Heat a skillet over medium heat, add butter, then soften the shallot for about 5 minutes without coloring.
3. Add vermouth and simmer, scraping the pan to dissolve any fond, until almost evaporated.
4. Add demi-glace cubes and simmer 5-7 minutes until melted and glossy.
5. Stir in cream and simmer 2-4 minutes until the sauce turns a rich medium brown.
6. Adjust consistency by reducing further if needed, then season with salt and pepper.
7. Strain through a fine mesh strainer and serve.
