# Jalapeno Popper Dip

## Overview

A creamy, cheesy dip with jalapeno heat and a big melt.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Dip
- Cuisine: American
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 20-30 minutes
- Total time: 30-40 minutes
- Serves: 8-10
- EGG setup: indirect
- EGG target temperature: moderate baking heat

## Ingredients

Dip:

- 32 oz cream cheese
- 3-4 cups cheddar cheese
- 1 cup mozzarella
- 1 cup parmesan
- 2-4 jalapenos, finely diced

## Method

1. Mix cream cheese, cheddar, mozzarella, parmesan, and jalapenos.
2. Spread in a baking dish and cook until hot and bubbling.
3. Rest briefly and serve warm.
