# Mexican Street Corn

## Overview

Grilled elote finished with a creamy lime rub, cheese, and herbs.

## Recipe Essentials

- Recipe type: Side
- Cuisine: Mexican
- Prep time: 5 minutes
- Cook time: 20 minutes
- Total time: 25 minutes
- Serves: 6

## Ingredients

- 6 ears of corn, shucked and cleaned
- 115 g (1/2 cup) mayonnaise
- 77 g (about 1/3 cup) of your favorite BBQ rub
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Grated cotija or Parmesan (cheddar works in a pinch)
- Fresh cilantro, chopped (reserve some for garnish)
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- Zest of 1 lime
- Lime wedges, to serve
- 10 g salted butter, cubed

## Method

1. Preheat grill to 400-450 F.
2. Grill corn, turning often, until lightly charred all over, about 10 minutes.
3. In a bowl, whisk mayonnaise, cilantro, garlic, rub, lime zest, and lime juice. Taste and adjust salt if needed.
4. Wrap corn in foil with butter and cook 6-8 minutes more until tender.
5. Open foil, brush with the crema, and sprinkle with cheese. Return to grill for 2-3 minutes.
6. Garnish with cilantro and serve with lime wedges.
