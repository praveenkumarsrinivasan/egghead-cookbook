# Sprits

## Overview

A simple spritz (sprits) blend for basting or spritzing during long cooks.

## Ingredients

- 1 L Kerri apple juice (clear)
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 cap apple cider vinegar
- 2-3 tbsp brisket mop or BBQ sauce of your choice
- Optional: Jack Daniel's Old No. 7 (a splash adds warmth)

## Method

1. Combine all ingredients and stir well.
2. Strain through a fine sieve (a coffee filter works well).
3. Pour into a spritz bottle and use as needed.
