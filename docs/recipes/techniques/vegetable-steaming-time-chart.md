## Doneness Guide

- **Crisp-Tender**  
  Bright colour, slight resistance, ideal for salads & sides

- **Tender**  
  Fully cooked but holds shape, ideal for most meals

- **Very Soft / Mash**  
  Breaks easily, suitable for mash, soups, baby food

---

## Steaming Tips

- Cut vegetables **uniformly**
- Add **dense vegetables first**, delicate ones later
- Avoid overcrowding the steamer
- Remove immediately to prevent carry-over cooking
- For Fozen, add about 10 mins. Frozen Sweet Potato total cook time 40 mins
