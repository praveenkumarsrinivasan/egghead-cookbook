## Doneness Guide

- **Lightly Roasted**  
  Just tender, minimal browning, fresh flavour

- **Well Roasted**  
  Caramelised edges, deep flavour, most common target

- **Very Soft / Jammy**  
  Collapsed texture, intense sweetness, great for purÃ©es & spreads

---

## Roasting Tips

- Use **high heat** for caramelisation
- Do **not overcrowd** the tray
- Flip halfway for even browning
- Salt after roasting for crisper edges
- Optional: finish with lemon, vinegar, or herbs
